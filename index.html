"Kesh Khash" — Graphic Designer & Photographer

I'm a passionate graphic designer dedicated to creating visually compelling and meaningful designs. I specialize in branding, poster design, and digital content that helps businesses and individuals communicate their ideas effectively.

With a strong eye for detail and creativity, I focus on delivering designs that are not only visually appealing but also purposeful and impactful. I enjoy turning concepts into clean, modern visuals that connect with audiences and leave a lasting impression.

I'm always learning, exploring new trends, and pushing my creativity to the next level. Based in Busia, Uganda — serving clients across the region and beyond.
